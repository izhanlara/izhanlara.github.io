About Me

I’m Izhan Lara, a web developer passionate about creating digital experiences that combine aesthetics, performance, and functionality. I love building clean yet distinctive interfaces and turning ideas into visually appealing projects.

I have experience working with React, Astro, JavaScript, PHP, Node.js, Angular, Astro, and Discord.js, as well as frontend technologies focused on modern design. I particularly enjoy crafting animated, responsive interfaces with attention to every detail.

I’ve developed projects such as OptiRuta (an application to optimize logistic routes), along with technical documentation for Notion and academic material to improve my future references. I also created Icon, a Discord bot that reached over 100 servers, automating moderation and social features.

Additionally, I have experience at ISB Instalaciones, a family business specializing in fire protection systems. During this time, I learned to configure VPNs, servers, backups, and develop the company’s website using WordPress.

Outside of coding, I’m passionate about cars, motorcycles, photography, and music. I’m always looking to learn something new and refine every detail of what I do.
